Philio’s expertise lies in the development of internet of things (IoT) and modern home automation.

Philio technology aims to develop consumer friendly products that provide security and energy saving for your home or business. Products include but are not limited to gateways, access control management, temperature sensors, humidity sensors, illumination, smoke sensors, gas sensors, and other. Philio utilizes different protocols such as Z-Wave, ZigBee, ULE, WiFi, LoRa and other related wireless technology product.
 
Philio is committed to provide good quality technology that’s affordable for everyone.

To report a bug or request additional devices, please create an issue at Github.
